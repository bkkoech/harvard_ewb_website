Get Involved

Thank you for your interest in Harvard EWB!

If you haven't already, contact us and request to join our email list! To get even faster access, you can also visit https://lists.hcs.harvard.edu/mailman/listinfo/ewb-announce and add yourself to the announce list. Here you'll get updates about our weekly meetings, project progress, and the wonderful special programming that Harvard EWB offers every semester

There are numerous ways you can contribute to Harvard EWB, some of which are listed below. Feel free to contact us for more details:

Students: Anyone is free to join, regardless of concentration or experience. You do not have to be an engineer. Our organization succeeds through the involvement of a diverse and dedicated group. Engineers, scientists, humanities majors, and more all play an integral part of our team.

Faculty: As Harvard EWB is still a young organization, we would greatly appreciate any recommendations or advice you are willing to give. We're always eager for more advisors, so please let us know if you are interested.

Other Harvard affiliates: Though Harvard EWB is technically affiliated with the College, we accept all members/advisors who are dedicated to EWB's mission. Graduate students of all disciplines are welcome to help with our work.

Professionals: We highly value the professionals we work with who assist us in reaching project goals. It can be as big or as small of a commitment as you would like; just let us know!

Businesses and Corporations: Harvard EWB needs funding to ensure its success in its current and future projects. Please consider becoming a sponsor of Harvard EWB.
