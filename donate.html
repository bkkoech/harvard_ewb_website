Individual Donations

You can help us raise money for our current project here. Our sponsorship packet containing information for sponsors can be here. Whether it's $5 or $5,000, your contribution will be instrumental to helping a community in need.

Corporate/University Sponsors

For Harvard EWB to continue its effort towards sustainable advances in developing communities, the organization needs sponsors to finance the necessary resources for its projects. The communities we work with are often in rural locations and severely economically disadvantaged so any and all financial assistance is welcome to give these communities the means to better themselves. A PDF containing information for sponsors can be found here.

Opportunities

Harvard EWB welcomes donations of all kinds, and as Harvard EWB holds an account with the nonprofit EWB-USA, all monetary donations towards Harvard EWB are tax-deductible. Donations need not be large, and can be conveniently done online here. EWB-USA will issue a tax receipt at the address given. If you are interested in donating by check instead, or are interested in supporting HUEWB through an alternative means (e.g. in-kind donations), please contact us at contact@harvardewb.org for more information.
